## Misc

### Change country in Google Play

Steps taken from
[here](https://www.guidingtech.com/change-country-in-google-play-store/),
section _Desktop_.

 1. [Payment Settings](https://payments.google.com/gp/w/home/settings) in
    Google Play.

 1. Click _Country/Region_.

 1. Choose option _Create new profile_.

 1. Press _Continue_ in the confirmation box to create a new profile.

 1. Select a country from the dropdown menu for Country/Region and continue.

 1. Enter your payment address and submit.
